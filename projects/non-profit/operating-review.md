# Non-Profit Operating Review

This is the recurring operating review surface for Non-Profit.

Cadence: monthly, plus event-driven review before board packets, grant deadlines, major donor communications, or real estate-sensitive decisions.

## Review Questions

### Mission and Program

- What mission priority led this period?
- Which programs advanced, stalled, or need revision?
- Which program decisions need leadership or board review?

### Funding and Stewardship

- Which donors, grants, reports, or pledges need action?
- Which restrictions or obligations are still unresolved?
- Which stewardship items are urgent or overdue?

### Asset and Governance

- Which real estate portfolio issues need review?
- Which risks require board, legal, accounting, or professional review?
- Which external claims or reports need stronger evidence?

### People and Delivery

- Which volunteer or partner commitments remain open?
- Which delivery pressures are creating mission or reporting risk?

### Next Actions

- What should leadership decide next?
- What should be prepared for board review?
- What should remain on hold pending stronger evidence or professional review?

### Turn Usefulness Review

- Which turn created the strongest leadership- or board-ready forward motion this cycle?
- Which turn looked complete but still hid cleanup in evidence, restrictions, or stewardship packaging?
- Where did the system stop too early and leave avoidable review reconstruction for leadership?
- Where did any turn get too close to implying donor commitments, external claims, or board action without approval?
- Which repeated failure mode should become a tighter worksheet, packet template, or governance gate?
- Where did a workflow look submission-ready but still stall on evidence, restrictions, spend, or approval authority?
