# Media Production Turn Exemplar

This worked example shows a production review turn that acts like a bounded workflow rather than a loose creative opinion.

## Input Context

Draft asset packet:

- one revised Grace Gems product graphic
- one caption draft
- one short contractor note about source imagery
- one unresolved question about whether a texture image is licensed for external use

## Orchestration Steps Performed Inside The Turn

1. Review the packet against the production brief and quality gate.
2. Separate aesthetic revision notes from rights-sensitive blocking issues.
3. Classify the packet as `Hold for clarification` rather than pushing it toward publication review.
4. Prepare the revision note and rights clarification request in one pass.
5. Stop before any publication, client commitment, or external-claim approval.

## Produced Artifacts Or Decisions

- review result: hold for clarification
- revision note for the visual packet
- explicit rights question for the unresolved source texture
- clean next-step handoff back into production

## Approval Stop Point

Human approval remains required for:

- publication or delivery
- rights decisions
- external claims
- any client-facing commitment about timing or usage

## Why This Counts As A Small Workflow Turn
