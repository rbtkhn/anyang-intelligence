# Media Production Membrane Notes

These notes describe how Media Production should transfer lessons while protecting client and rights-sensitive context.

## Transfer Boundary

Allowed transfer:

- generic quality-gate patterns
- contractor-governance patterns
- abundance review questions
- generic production workflow lessons

Blocked transfer without explicit human review:

- client-specific private briefs
- unpublished assets
- Grace Gems product claims and project-specific language
- rights-sensitive source material or third-party assets

## Private Boundary

Private or lane-local material includes:

- client assets and drafts
- unpublished scripts, visual concepts, and review notes
- contractor private details and payment/tax records
- source material or rights questions that are not resolved

## Working Rule

Media Production may transfer generic process patterns.

It may not transfer private client context, private contractor context, or rights-sensitive material without explicit approval.
