# Creative Production Operator 14-Day Guided Ramp

Entry: prerequisite skills test `Pass` and authorized onboarding entry condition satisfied.

This ramp uses synthetic exercises and approved public sources only. It does not authorize client access, spending, publication, delivery, customer contact, or live assignment. Use the assignment gate before later Grace Gems or Predictive History work.

| Day | Focus | Evidence |
| ---: | --- | --- |
| 1 | Purpose, role boundaries, privacy, and authority | Boundary acknowledgment |
| 2 | File conventions, Drive sharing, repository proof, and review packets | Synthetic folder and manifest |
| 3 | Public-source reading and source-bound briefing | Brief and do-not-invent list |
| 4 | Distinct concepts and recommendation | Three concepts |
| 5 | Production planning and risk identification | Production plan |
| 6 | First synthetic/public-source draft | Editable draft and notes |
| 7 | Midpoint review and correction plan | Trainer review receipt |
| 8 | Creative Abundance Quality Gate | Completed quality review |
| 9 | Rights, claims, and uncertainty handling | Rights/source log |
| 10 | Review-ready handoff packaging | Files, decision request, next action |
| 11 | One bounded revision | Before/after evidence |
| 12 | Repurposing a synthetic idea | Two related outputs and reuse note |
| 13 | Final synthetic production packet | Complete packet |
| 14 | Trainer review and next-step decision | Ramp completion receipt |

Every submission states what was made, source boundary, tool/AI disclosure, uncertainties, time used, decision needed, and next action. Stop for `Hold` when authority, source, rights, sharing, budget, or scope is unclear.

Completion decisions: `Complete — ready for bounded assignment review`, `Complete with conditions`, `Extend ramp`, or `Do not advance`. Completion does not assign work.
