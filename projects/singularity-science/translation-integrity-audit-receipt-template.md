## Measurement rules

- Label every time or cost figure as observed, reconstructed, or estimated.
- Do not count hypothetical avoided work as realized savings.
- Record quality, rights, privacy, authority, and human-burden guardrails alongside speed or volume.
- A workflow cannot receive pass solely because it is faster or cheaper.
- A concern or blocked result must name the next action.
