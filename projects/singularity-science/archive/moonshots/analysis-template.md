## ROI Disposition Rules

- `primitive-candidate`: reusable gate, checklist, template, or validator that could apply across at least 2 lanes or is likely to recur across Moonshots episodes
- `needs-verification`: highest-value implication depends on unstable benchmark, company, policy, pricing, or timeline claims
- `lane-test-ready`: can be translated into a lane-safe review question or checklist without making a new factual claim
- `worldview-only`: strategically interesting but not yet reusable or safe to route
- `preserved`: already converted into a repo-visible primitive or routing artifact
