## Newsletter Text

The Singularity is discovering that intelligence, pushed far enough, bends back to face itself. Jurgen Schmidhuber argues that the one constant across training and rollouts is the model itself, so a mind should learn to model itself to better predict its next token.

If self-knowledge is the frontier, orchestration is the near ground, and a hobbyist reverse-engineered Sakana's Fugu into tinyrouter, a ~10k-parameter conductor that beat every model on MMLU by handing each question to the right specialist. Raw scale still matters, as Google's rumored Gemini 3.5 Pro may ship a 2-million-token context, doubling Anthropic's Opus and Fable.

On Fable itself, Google's Andy Coenen credits three ingredients, sheer scale, relentless long-horizon agentic RL, and a constitution that turns thousands of decisions into one legible compass.
