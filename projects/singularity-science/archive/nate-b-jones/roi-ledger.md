## ROI Disposition Rules

| Disposition | Use when |
| --- | --- |
| `primitive-candidate` | The seam yields a reusable gate, checklist, template, or validator that could apply across at least 2 lanes or is likely to recur across Nate B. Jones or adjacent Singularity Science sources |
