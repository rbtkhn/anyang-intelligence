## Source Discipline

- Keep source metadata separate from analysis.
- Treat interview claims as claims, not doctrine.
- Preserve transcript bulk only inside the archive.
- Pull out seams, primitives, and verification queues.
- Route only translated questions or review gates across customer membranes.
