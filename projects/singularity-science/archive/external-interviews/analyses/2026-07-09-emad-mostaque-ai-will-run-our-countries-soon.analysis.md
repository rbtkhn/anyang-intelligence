## Seam Analysis

### Seam: sovereign access to intelligence

- Core claim: intelligence is becoming a strategic resource that could be gated by companies, governments, licensing, compute access, or political compliance.
- Likely durable:
  - access to frontier capability is a governance and sovereignty issue
  - concentration of compute, model access, and policy control can shape who can act
  - Anyang should distinguish access rights from capability hype
- Likely speculative:
  - exact timelines for licensing regimes
  - exact degree of provider or state concentration
  - specific political tests or enforcement mechanisms
- Verification burden: high
- Primitive opportunity: intelligence-access rights review gate for evaluating whether a workflow depends on revocable, politically sensitive, or provider-controlled intelligence access
- Lane-safe routing opportunity: not yet; preserve as a primitive candidate inside Singularity Science
- Membrane risk: high; political forecasts can turn into doctrine too quickly
- ROI disposition: primitive-candidate

### Seam: personhood and legal category pressure

- Core claim: AI personhood debates should not be reduced to capability because replication, persistence, voting, legal rights, and human lineage create category collisions.
- Likely durable:
  - capability alone is a fragile basis for personhood or authority
  - replication and persistence create governance problems different from ordinary human personhood
  - legal and moral categories need explicit definitions before high-stakes claims are made
- Likely speculative:
  - the speaker's preferred taxonomy
  - which rights regimes will actually emerge
  - how quickly AI legal status will become operational
- Verification burden: medium
- Primitive opportunity: nonhuman-agency rights taxonomy prompt for research discussions, not legal advice
- Lane-safe routing opportunity: yes, but only as a discussion or research structuring question
- Membrane risk: medium; legal language must not become advice
- ROI disposition: lane-test-ready
