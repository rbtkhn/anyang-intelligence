# Singularity Science Research Operating Model

This document preserves the research-team choices for the purpose, structure, cadence, outputs, and governance of Singularity Science.

It is the operating model for the research lane. It does not replace source notes, transcript analyses, the archive, or human judgment.

## Purpose

Primary purpose: **product intelligence**.

Singularity Science exists to translate singularity-relevant sources into Anyang Intelligence product primitives, skills, loops, templates, checklists, and governance warnings.

The research project may build a serious internal body of synthesis over time, but the first job is not general commentary. The first job is to improve Anyang Intelligence's judgment and operating substrate.

## Primary Customer

Primary customer: **the research team**.

The research team needs structured source analysis, ledgers, review cadence, and a disciplined way to convert research into operating implications.

Anyang Intelligence and customer lanes may benefit from the output, but Singularity Science should first serve the research team's ability to see, compare, and interpret source material.

## Source Scope

Source scope: **research canon**.

Singularity Science should archive sources that build a long-term knowledge base for the project.

This includes Innermost Loop and Moonshots, but source eligibility is not limited to those streams. A source is worth archiving when it helps build durable understanding of AI, agency, cognition, media, science, markets, governance, or human formation.

## Primary Output

Primary output: **analysis memos**.

Source notes and research ledgers matter, but the most important output is the analysis memo: a deeper synthesis of signal, implications, governance risk, and primitive candidates.

Each analysis memo should answer:

- What is the core research signal?
- Why does it matter?
- What is hype versus durable signal?
- What does it imply for Anyang Intelligence?
- Which governance boundary is stressed?
- What product primitive, if any, might be needed?

## Structure

Preferred structure: **split by domain now**.

Singularity Science should preserve source streams such as Innermost Loop and Moonshots, but the research project should also be ready to organize by domain.

Initial domain structure:

- AI and agents
- Cognition and learning
- Media and synthetic culture
- Science and technology
- Markets and organizations
- Governance and authority
- Human formation

The archive can keep source-stream folders, while analyses and ledgers should tag or route by domain.

## Relationship To Customer Lanes

Relationship: **advisory**.

Singularity Science may inform customer lanes, but it does not create customer obligations directly.

Research output may suggest implications for Media Production, Grace Gems, Learning Core, retired Non-Profit project, Mountain Villa, or Book Club. Those implications remain advisory until a human operator chooses to route them through the relevant customer membrane.

## Governance Priority

Primary governance risk: **authority leak**.

The main risk is turning research interpretation into customer action without human approval.

Singularity Science should be especially careful when research touches:

- children or education
- money, pricing, fundraising, or business commitments
- legal, tax, nonprofit, board, or compliance questions
- publication or public claims
- rights, attribution, transcript reuse, or source licensing
- cross-customer transfer

The system may recommend. Humans decide whether to preserve, route, publish, or act.

## Cadence

Cadence: **event-driven**.

Analyze sources when they arrive or when a research signal matters. Do not force a weekly synthesis before there is enough signal.

Use weekly or monthly synthesis later only if source volume or decision pressure justifies it.

## First 30-Day Success Criterion

First success criterion: **customer-impact map**.

The first 30 days should produce a map showing how Singularity Science informs each Anyang Intelligence lane without creating obligations or leaking source context.

The map should answer:

- What kinds of research signals matter to each lane?
- Which lanes are most exposed to singularity-relevant change?
- What governance boundaries are most likely to be stressed?
- What primitive candidates appear repeatedly?
- What should remain inside Singularity Science?

## Preservation Decision

The elicitation result should be preserved as this `research-operating-model.md`.

Future updates should revise this document when the research team's purpose, structure, cadence, output standard, or governance boundary changes.

## Boundary

Do not treat this operating model as permission to publish, route, or act on source-derived claims.

Singularity Science turns source material into analysis and implications. Human authority decides what becomes customer work, public work, product doctrine, or reusable infrastructure.
