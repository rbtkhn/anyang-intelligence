## Strategic Roadmap

Anyang Intelligence's strategic direction is **embodied agency**: governed intelligence that helps people understand, coordinate, and eventually act in digital and physical worlds. Capability expands only when human approval, evidence, safety, privacy, security, and accountability remain legible.

| Horizon | Operating focus | Required proof before moving forward |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Now | Governed digital intelligence: decision support, memory, coordination, and learning | Useful work, visible approvals, evidence, and review cadence in active projects |
| Next | Tool and workflow coordination through bounded, reversible automation | Clear task scope, accountable owner, exception handling, receipts, and human override |
| By July 2028 | Conditional embodied capability | A useful, safe, maintainable, economically justified system that meets privacy, security, insurance, support, and override requirements |

### By July 2028: Useful Humanoid Robot

Anyang Intelligence intends to evaluate and, if the decision gates are met, purchase a useful cutting-edge humanoid robot by July 2028. This is a long-range operating intention, not an approved purchase, budgeted obligation, or claim about a particular vendor or capability.

The purchase decision must remain owner-approved and be supported by evidence that the specific system is useful for defined work, safe and maintainable in its intended environment, economically justified, and workable with required privacy, security, insurance, and support boundaries. No project may assume the robot, commit funds, or delegate safety-critical decisions to it before that review.

### Through July 2028: China Humanoid-Market Attention

China is a top-three strategic watch-and-relationship market for Anyang Intelligence during the next two years. The purpose is to learn from robotics deployment, component supply, pricing, and vendor/service maturity; establish carefully vetted relationships with relevant suppliers, integrators, researchers, and market-entry advisors; and preserve a credible sourcing option for a future humanoid evaluation.

This is not a commitment to enter China, purchase Chinese equipment, transfer intellectual property, or create a concentrated supplier dependency. Any China-related engagement must remain vendor-neutral, reversible, and subject to export-control, tariff, data-governance, cybersecurity, intellectual-property, contract, and professional-review requirements. Core Anyang Intelligence IP, decision authority, and sensitive child/family, property, financial, or project data stay outside a China-dependent operating path.

Use [China Humanoid Market Watch](singularity-science/china-humanoid-market-watch.md) for the quarterly intelligence and relationship cadence.
