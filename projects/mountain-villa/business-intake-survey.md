# Mountain Villa Phase 1 Business Intake

## Purpose

This is the Mountain Villa project-specific question set for explicit `$business-intake create` use. It supports an owner-governed initial business context for property stewardship and a possible sale. It does not assume rental, hospitality, guest use, listing, broker engagement, or any other commercial action.

Do not ask the business questions until the shared authority and data gate is `Confirmed`. Ask them in batches of no more than three, reflect what was heard, and use `Missing` rather than inventing property or business facts.

Do not place completed answers or packets in Git. Exclude or redact access instructions, security details, exact vulnerabilities, owner contact details, insurance identifiers, buyer or contractor identities, account records, and other restricted property information. Use approved opaque external references when evidence must remain outside the repository.

## Batch 1: Goal, Operating Model, And Evidence

1. **Goal and intended decision:** What outcome should Mountain Villa support over the next 30-90 days, and what exact decision should the first operating review prepare? State what is outside scope.

2. **Asset, stakeholders, and channels:** Which owner-approved uses, sale-readiness activities, services, stakeholders, and channels are relevant now? Distinguish current facts from possible future sale, rental, retreat, broker, buyer, contractor, or guest activity.

3. **Economics and evidence quality:** What owner-approved evidence is available for carrying costs, maintenance estimates, improvement budgets, target economics, or sale preparation? Label estimates and missing values. Route tax, accounting, insurance, legal, appraisal, disclosure, and financial classifications to the appropriate professional or governance workflow.

## Batch 2: Signals, Capacity, And Authority

4. **Operating signals:** What recent redacted patterns from inspections, maintenance, seasonal events, utilities, access, contractors, owner use, or sale preparation should affect the first review? Provide summaries or opaque references, not restricted property details or raw private records.

5. **Capacity and pause conditions:** What realistic owner time, budget, contractor availability, seasonal windows, access constraints, professional-review dependencies, and weekly work capacity govern the next month? What conditions require work to pause?

6. **Owner decision rules:** Which decisions require explicit owner or professional approval? Include property access, safety, spending, contractors, insurance, listing, pricing, broker engagement, disclosures, buyer or guest communication, sale commitments, rental, publication, and source changes.

## Completion Boundary

After the six evidence groups are reviewed, `$business-intake create` may produce only the shared governed packets and a readiness state of `Ready`, `Provisional`, or `Hold`. Context approval does not make it effective. An approved packet remains `Awaiting Persistence` until an authorized operator confirms external tenant-private preservation.

The first Mountain Villa operating review requires separate owner authorization. No intake answer authorizes property access, publication, pricing, spending, hiring, contractor work, buyer or guest communication, safety decisions, listing, rental, sale commitments, or source changes.
