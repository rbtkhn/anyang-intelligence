# Learning Core Nested-Loop Authority Review

Status: `Prepared — do not apply to live family workflow yet`

This is the final-stage review for applying a cross-lane primitive to Learning Core. It is not an authorization to change a learner profile, plan, parent-facing message, saved record, or child-facing experience.

## Required confirmations

- Parent/guardian authority remains canonical.
- Approved evidence and preservation boundaries are current.
- Observations remain distinct from interpretations.
- Learner agency is represented without weakening parent authority.
- Privacy, deletion, saving, and sharing rules are explicit.
- The service or tool does not become owner of the family learning loop.
- The candidate primitive does not create child-facing, educational, clinical, legal, or institutional authority.
- The test uses only approved evidence and a bounded artifact.

## Proposed bounded test

Apply the candidate primitive first to a review of one existing Learning Core artifact—such as the monthly portfolio review or parent-intake readiness packet—without mutating the artifact.

Assess whether the primitive improves:

- evidence clarity;
- parent decision visibility;
- distinction between observation and interpretation;
- selective continuity;
- family independence;
- next-cycle usefulness.

## Decision options

- `Hold for missing authority`
- `Review exercise only`
- `Approved bounded artifact test`
- `Reject transfer`

Default: `Hold for missing authority` until the specific artifact, authority receipt, and approved evidence are named.
