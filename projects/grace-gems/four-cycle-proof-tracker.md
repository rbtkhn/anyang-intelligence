# Grace Gems Four-Cycle Proof Tracker

Status: `Blocked pending owner-approved intake`

This tracker is prepared for four counted weekly proof cycles. Public-signal preflight does not count as Cycle 1.

## Start gate

Start only when all are present:

- owner-approved priority listings or products;
- approved product and policy sources;
- named owner, reviewer, assignee, and approval scopes;
- claim and rights boundaries;
- approved deliverables;
- delivery evidence path;
- permitted public-signal use.

## Evidence rules

- Blocked cycles remain blocked.
- Synthetic or public-signal work cannot be relabeled as commercial proof.
- Claim-bearing work requires exact-version approval and delivery evidence.
- Customer-support raw messages remain outside Git and private.
- Unknown metrics remain `Unknown`, not zero.

## Exit decision

After four completed live cycles, prepare an evidence-backed day-90 decision. Do not claim commercial improvement without visible economics, approval receipts, delivery evidence, and honest outcome limits.
