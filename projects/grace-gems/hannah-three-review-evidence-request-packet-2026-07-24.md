### 1. Shipping-origin accuracy

**Maximum sample:** 10 recent completed orders selected to represent both
jewelry segments and each materially different fulfillment path.

**Minimum fields:**

- opaque order-to-shipment reference;
- carrier name;
- first customer-leg carrier-acceptance date, city, state, and country;
- storefront or segment category;
- fulfillment-path category;
- public ship-from wording applicable at the time of review.

**Exclude:**

- customer names, addresses, or messages;
- complete tracking or order numbers;
- payment details;
- supplier identities;
- raw screenshots or documents.

### 2. Personalization intake

**Maximum sample:** Five recent custom inquiries or accepted orders, with no
more than one owner-selected example per eligible fine-jewelry shop when
available.

**Minimum fields:**

- redacted request summary;
- assigned service category;
- opaque base-design reference and requested changes;
- applicable gemstone, material, size, element, and quantity fields;
- feasibility decision;
- price and lead-time acknowledgment status, without private economics;
- customer-approval status before production;
- whether an AI concept was used;
- whether a human-approved production specification replaced the concept.

**Exclude:**

- customer identity or contact details;
- full messages;
- payment information;
- personal images;
- proprietary customer artwork;
- production-ready design files.

### 3. Supplier-dependent fulfillment

**Maximum sample:** Eight recent completed affordable-shop orders selected to
represent materially different supplier or fulfillment paths.

**Minimum fields:**

- redacted storefront and product-reference category;
- customer-order timestamp;
- supplier-availability confirmation timestamp;
- supplier-purchase timestamp;
- United States receipt or quality-check milestone when applicable;
- promised and actual customer-dispatch dates;
- sanitized exception category.

**Exclude:**

- customer or supplier identities;
- addresses, messages, or full order numbers;
- invoices, unit costs, margins, or payment data;
- complete tracking numbers.
