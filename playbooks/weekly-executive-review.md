# Weekly Executive Review

Use [Analytical Interfaces](../docs/analytical-interfaces.md) for the review title, lead judgment, controlling object, decision questions, and uncertainty causes. Run `.\tools\run.ps1 project validate-interfaces` when the output is a governed publication or decision surface.

## Purpose

Create a recurring leadership surface that answers what changed, what matters, and what needs action.

## Agenda

- Current priority review.
- Metrics narrative.
- Project and owner updates.
- Open decisions.
- Blockers and dependencies.
- Risk review.
- Follow-ups and next actions.

## Output

The review should produce:

- Updated decision log.
- Updated project tracker.
- Updated risk register.
- Confirmed next actions.
- Notes for the learning loop.

## Standard

The review should be short enough to use every week and substantial enough to prevent leadership drift.
