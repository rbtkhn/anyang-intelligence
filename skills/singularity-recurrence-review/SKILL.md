# Singularity Recurrence Review

Use after `singularity-intake` completes its transcript, source note, analysis, and canonical ledger update. Review one completed intake at a time.

This skill is an archive-learning pass. It does not fetch sources, move transcript bulk, create primitives, publish doctrine, route customer work, alter a canonical ledger, or change authority.

## Required inputs

- one completed intake artifact set;
- the lane's canonical ledger;
- prior source notes and analyses;
- source attribution, provenance, rights status, and lane identity.

Return `Hold` when source identity, provenance, rights status, or lane mapping is ambiguous.

## Review method

1. Read the new source note and analysis first.
2. Extract each seam or research signal exactly as the lane analysis names it.
3. Compare each seam with prior ledger rows and linked analyses.
4. Assess whether apparent matches are independent, copied, dependent, merely rhetorical, or genuinely divergent.
5. Record recurrence count, evidence strength, uncertainty, and the next evidence needed.
6. Assign exactly one disposition per seam: `watch`, `verify`, `preserve-candidate`, `lane-test-ready`, or `drop`.
7. Produce a derived recurrence packet and stop.

## Independence rules

- Do not count copied coverage, shared press release language, or dependent commentary as independent recurrence.
- Repeated rhetoric is not verification.
- A recurring mechanism may be stronger than a recurring prediction, benchmark, or timeline claim.
- Preserve source-specific worldview and unresolved conflict instead of flattening it into a consensus.

## Required output

The recurrence packet must include:

- review ID and date;
- source and lane identifiers;
- matched seam identifiers and links;
- source-independence assessment;
- recurrence count;
- strengthening, weakening, or divergence judgment;
- evidence strength and uncertainty cause;
- exactly one disposition per seam;
- evidence gap and next action;
- human authority required;
- explicit statement of what remains inside Singularity Science;
- performance fields: comparison minutes, duplicate-seam findings, corrections, and time to next action.

Do not modify the canonical ledger during this skill. The packet is a derived review artifact for `singularity-learning-update`.

## ROI baseline

For the first five comparable reviews, record:

- minutes spent comparing the new source with prior work;
- duplicate-seam discoveries;
- repeated analysis or ledger corrections;
- premature candidates later held or rejected;
- time from intake completion to a clear next action.

The first-wave ROI gate passes only if at least one approved threshold is met without increased rights risk, attribution error, lane confusion, review burden, or unsupported promotion.

## Boundary

Recurrence is a reason to investigate, not permission to believe. This skill cannot create doctrine, preserve a primitive, authorize a lane test, or create customer/project state.
