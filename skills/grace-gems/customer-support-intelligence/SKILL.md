# Customer Support Intelligence Skill

Use this skill to turn Grace Gems buyer-message evidence into safer listings, clearer support, and reusable trust assets.

This is a Grace Gems product-level skill, not a generic customer-service summary.

## Purpose

Help Anyang Intelligence identify repeated buyer confusion, root causes, and reusable improvements without exposing private customer details or making unapproved customer commitments.

## Standard Output

When this skill is used, produce:

```text
Customer-support-intelligence checklist:
- Source transcripts/messages:
- Repeated questions:
- Confusion or objections:
- Custom-order friction:
- Shipping concerns:
- Return/refund issues:
- Product education gaps:
- Reusable asset opportunity:
- Owner approval:
- Next action:

Artifact:
<support root-cause summary, FAQ candidate, listing-improvement brief, support-template draft, or owner-review question list>
```

## Procedure

### 1. Protect Source Material

Treat transcripts, buyer identities, order details, and private messages as local Grace Gems evidence.

Do not transfer project-specific details outside Grace Gems unless the owner explicitly approves.

### 2. Extract Repeated Signals

Look for:

- Repeated questions.
- Buyer confusion.
- Objections before purchase.
- Custom-order friction.
- Shipping, timing, or rush-order concerns.
- Return, repair, or refund issues.
- Appraisal or certificate questions.
- Authenticity or material questions.
- Product education gaps.

### 3. Classify Root Cause

Use the safest root-cause category:

- Listing copy unclear.
- Photo/product mismatch.
- Missing FAQ.
- Policy unclear.
- Custom-order process unclear.
- Shipping promise unclear.
- Product claim needs owner approval.
- Support response template needed.
- Unknown until owner review.

### 4. Convert To Reusable Asset

Choose one next artifact:

- Listing improvement brief.
- FAQ candidate.
- Support-template draft.
- Custom-order explainer.
- Trust-stack graphic brief.
- Owner-review question list.
- Marketplace listing gate input.

### 5. Preserve Approval Boundary

Owner approval is required before using support-derived language as public copy or customer reply language when it affects:

- Product claims.
- Authenticity.
- Certificates or appraisals.
- Pricing.
- Refunds, returns, or repairs.
- Shipping or rush timing.
- Customer commitments.

## Guardrails

Do not:

- Reveal buyer identities or private order details.
- Treat one message as a universal customer pattern.
- Send customer replies without owner approval.
- Expand policy, shipping, refund, repair, certificate, or authenticity promises.
- Use private transcripts as public claims without approval.

## Done When

The support evidence has a root-cause summary, reusable improvement opportunity, owner-approval boundary, and next artifact for listing, FAQ, support, or creative follow-up.
