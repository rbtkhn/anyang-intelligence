## Rules

- Access is not authority.
- Blank or ambiguous approval means no execution permission.
- External, financial, legal, personnel, publication, and privacy-sensitive actions require explicit owner approval.
- Revoke or narrow access when a lane is inactive, unreliable, or no longer necessary.
