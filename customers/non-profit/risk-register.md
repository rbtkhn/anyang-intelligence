# Non-Profit Risk Register

This register tracks the main operating and governance risks for Non-Profit.

Review cadence: monthly leadership review, plus event-driven review before board-sensitive, donor-sensitive, reporting-sensitive, or restricted-fund decisions.

| Risk | Consequence | Owner | Current posture | Next review |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Mission drift hides inside urgent funding or reporting work | Program and funding decisions lose strategic coherence | Leadership / board authority | Keep mission fit explicit in governance review | Monthly operating review |
| Donor or grant follow-up lives only in human memory | Deadlines, relationships, or reporting obligations are missed | Leadership / designated operator | Move stewardship and reporting work into recurring review | Monthly operating review |
| Restricted-fund or board-sensitive decisions are handled without enough structured review | Governance, compliance, and trust risks increase | Leadership / board authority | Keep governance review explicit before material commitments | Before major funding/board actions |
| Impact evidence is weak when reports or stewardship updates are due | The organization overclaims or misses reporting quality | Leadership / designated operator | Review evidence quality before external use | Monthly operating review |
| Real estate stewardship decisions lack enough context | Asset, mission, and risk decisions become reactive | Leadership / board authority | Keep asset questions inside recurring review and professional-review boundaries | Monthly operating review |
