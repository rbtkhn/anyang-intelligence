# Non-Profit 30-Day Plan

This plan creates the first recurring support surfaces for Non-Profit.

## Week 1: Governance Baseline

- Create the first risk register and decision log.
- Confirm the mission, program, donor, grant, and real-estate context map from the install.
- Identify the next leadership and board review needs.

## Week 2: Stewardship and Reporting Review

- Review open donor, grant, reporting, and restriction-sensitive items.
- Identify missing evidence, weak follow-up, and overdue stewardship work.
- Prepare the first recurring stewardship review notes.

## Week 3: Asset and Risk Review

- Review real estate stewardship, obligations, and current portfolio questions.
- Update the risk register based on governance and delivery pressure.
- Identify which questions need board or professional review.

## Week 4: Operating Review and Next Cycle

- Run the first monthly operating review.
- Update the decision log from real mission, donor, and asset pressure.
- Choose the next 30-day focus: programs, stewardship, reporting, or board preparation.
