# Non-Profit Membrane Notes

These notes describe how Non-Profit should transfer lessons while protecting donor, beneficiary, board, and compliance-sensitive context.

## Transfer Boundary

Allowed transfer:

- generic board-review patterns
- donor-stewardship review questions
- restricted-fund caution patterns
- impact-evidence and asset-governance discipline

Blocked transfer without explicit human approval:

- donor identities or donor-specific restrictions
- beneficiary-sensitive stories or evidence
- board deliberations
- draft external claims or compliance-sensitive details

## Private Boundary

Private or lane-local material includes:

- donor notes, communications, and restrictions
- beneficiary or community-sensitive evidence
- board disagreements or deliberations
- real estate, valuation, tax, legal, and compliance-sensitive records

## Working Rule

Non-Profit may transfer generic governance and stewardship patterns.

It may not transfer private donor, beneficiary, board, or compliance-sensitive context without explicit human approval.
