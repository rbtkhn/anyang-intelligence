# Singularity Science Operating Review

This is the recurring operating review surface for the Singularity Science research lane.

Cadence:

- Weekly when multiple source captures or primitive candidates are active
- Event-driven when a source creates a new governance warning or cross-lane routing pressure

## Review Questions

### Source Flow

- What sources were captured, analyzed, or skipped?
- Which source lanes produced the strongest signals?
- Where is source-note or analysis follow-up missing?

### Signal Quality

- What looked durable versus hype?
- Which claims still need primary verification before routing?
- Which analyses produced clear product or governance implications?

### Primitive Progress

- Which primitive candidates appeared repeatedly?
- Which candidates were preserved, tested, or left at watch status?
- Which primitives still need a receiving-lane test?

### Routing and Membranes

- Which customer lanes appear affected?
- What stayed correctly inside Singularity Science?
- Did any implication require stronger transfer language or explicit hold status?

### Turn Usefulness Review

- Which research turn created the clearest product or governance forward motion this cycle?
- Which turn looked complete but still required hidden lane-translation cleanup?
- Where did the system stop too early and leave avoidable routing work for the operator?
- Where did a turn get too close to turning research into lane doctrine without membrane review?
- Which repeated failure mode should become a tighter primitive, template, or routing guardrail?

### Next Cycle

- What source should be analyzed next?
- What primitive should be tightened next?
- What lane-safe question, if any, is ready for review elsewhere?
