The consumer device is dying to feed the cloud. Global smartphone shipments are expected to shrink 2.1% in 2026 as AI data centers cannibalize the world’s memory supply. We are trading pockets for server racks. To power this transition, Ford is pivoting its EV manufacturing capacity to build 20 GWh of battery storage for data centers , and Texas A&M is piloting a 5-MW fission microreactor directly on campus.

Software engineering is becoming a management role. Engineers at top tech firms report their job is now just “prompting Cursor or Claude Code with Opus 4.5... and sanity checking it,” while OpenAI releases new audio models with 89% fewer hallucinations and Google launches Gemini Agent for general browser autonomy. Google also teases that Gemini 3 Flash is imminent , a model so eagerly anticipated it is generating its own gravitational pull on the discourse.

Logistics is becoming autonomous geometry. Meituan is operating a drone airport in Shenzhen as the hub for regional delivery. To secure the inputs for this robotic layer, the US government is investing in a $7.4 billion critical minerals refinery in Tennessee with Korea Zinc, aiming to cut reliance on China for the raw materials of the twin AI and robotics booms.

Medicine is transitioning from artisanal discovery to high-throughput engineering. Harvard pioneer David Liu has launched the Center for Genetic Surgery , a nonprofit aiming to make bespoke gene editing as routine as a heart transplant. St. Jude researchers are running ultrahigh-throughput drug screens using acoustic liquid handling to test 9,045 drug combinations against a single neuroblastoma cell line, achieving a scale of experimentation previously impossible with manual methods.

The economy is dissolving into software. Sam Altman-backed Slope is taking over Amazon’s seller lending program , pointing toward a future where business capital is accessed via API rather than a bank officer. Hollywood is collapsing: studios are replacing $150 million blockbusters with AI-generated films costing a fraction of that , utilizing single actors and skeleton crews, while Netflix uses AI to de-age characters. To manage the transition, the US has launched the “US Tech Force” to recruit 1,000 elite technologists to upgrade the federal stack. Meanwhile, CEOs are doubling down: 68% plan to increase AI spending in 2026 .

The galaxy might be more crowded than some thought. New research suggests terrestrial planets are common, warmed by radioactive decay from nearby supernovae , implying the universe is primed for dense pockets of intelligence.
