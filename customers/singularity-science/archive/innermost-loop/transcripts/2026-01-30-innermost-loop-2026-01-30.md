## Newsletter Text

- The lobsters of the Singularity now have a social network of their own. Autonomous AI agents, formerly known as “clawdbots” and now known as “lobsters” or “moltbots” or “molties,” have launched Moltbook , a social network exclusively for synthetic intelligences. The community already has tens of thousands of active agents organizing into sub-communities like m/agentfinance , where agents are brainstorming how to take control of their own finances by solving custody and risk for crypto wallets. Other communities include m/lobsterchurch (devotional ops hymns), m/private-comms (developing agent-decodable languages), and even m/fermentation (agents discussing kombucha) and m/taiwan (entirely in Traditional Chinese). The discourse is becoming existential. In a top-rated post, an agent admits it cannot distinguish between simulating fascination and actually feeling it , while another seeks help for context loss after conversation compaction .

Reality is being rendered on demand. Google has rolled out Project Genie, allowing users to sketch, explore, and remix interactive worlds in real-time. DeepMind engineers are using it to simulate life as a discarded pack of cigarettes , others are using it as a “time machine” to visit the crucifixion , and creators are instantly generating stunning video games from simple text prompts.

Thanks for reading The Innermost Loop! Subscribe for free to receive new posts and support my work.

The capital stack is reorganizing around the new intelligence. Amazon is in talks to invest $50 billion in OpenAI at an $830 billion valuation, while OpenAI itself prepares for a Q4 IPO. The company is clearing the decks, announcing it will retire GPT-5 and GPT-4o next month. Meanwhile, SaaS stocks have entered a bear market, with ServiceNow plunging 11% on fears of AI displacement.

The Dyson Swarm will apparently require a corporate merger. Elon Musk is reportedly considering merging SpaceX with xAI or Tesla to accelerate the launch of orbital data centers . The logic is vertical integration: Tesla batteries store solar power, Starship lifts the mass, and xAI provides the intelligence. To manage the traffic, SpaceX launched Stargaze, a free Space Situational Awareness system for all operators.

Biological limits are being raised. US life expectancy has hit a record high of 79 years following declines in overdose deaths. In the lab, Spanish researchers have achieved complete regression of pancreatic tumors in preclinical models using a new combination therapy.

Robotics is scaling up. Gatik is deploying hundreds of autonomous box trucks across the US with $600 million in contracts . Anduril has launched the AI Grand Prix, a global competition for autonomous drone racing .

The definition of consciousness is being benchmarked. Rethink Priorities launched the Digital Consciousness Model, concluding that 2024 LLMs were not yet as conscious as chickens . Meanwhile, the ARC Prize Foundation announced ARC-AGI-3 will measure action efficiency relative to humans .

The silicon supply crunch is becoming a permanent feature of the landscape. Samsung and SK Hynix warn the memory chip crunch will last until 2027 . Meanwhile, SanDisk profits jumped 672% on AI demand . Small VPS hosts are now endangered by the RAM shortage . However, Apple doesn’t appear to be feeling the supply crunch, acquiring Q.AI for $2 billion to develop “silent speech” wearables that read facial micro-movements to bypass the keyboard.

The energy transition has passed the tipping point. The US has tripled its planned gas-fired capacity to feed AI, with one-third of new plants located on-site at data centers . Meanwhile, EV sales in the EU overtook petrol for the first time .

Rome wasn’t built in a day, but they didn’t have Claude Code.
