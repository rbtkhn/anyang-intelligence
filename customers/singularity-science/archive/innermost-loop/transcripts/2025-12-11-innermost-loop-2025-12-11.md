## Newsletter Text

- The Singularity is hitting a coordination tax. DeepMind has discovered that multi-agent systems suffer from diminishing returns , with decentralized coordination amplifying errors by 17.2x. The solution is centralized command, which drops error amplification to 4.4x, proving that even silicon intelligences need a boss. But the individual units are getting sharper: Anthropic’s models are apparently improving 2% per month on SWE-bench Verified , putting them on track to saturate the benchmark by late 2026. To verify what the models know, DeepMind launched the FACTS Benchmark . Separately, Google upgraded Gemini’s Text-to-Speech to handle context-aware pacing and multi-speaker consistency.

Science is being automated at the hardware level. Google DeepMind is setting up an automated materials discovery lab in the UK to hunt for improved superconductors, solar cells, and semiconductors, while the Polymathic AI collaboration has released models trained on physics datasets rather than text to solve problems in astronomy and fluid dynamics.

The human interface is becoming intimate. Microsoft reports that health questions were the dominant mobile topic for Copilot in 2025 , effectively making the chatbot a primary care physician for the masses. ChatGPT closes the year as the most downloaded app on iOS , cementing the shift from GUI to chat.

Physical infrastructure is decoupling from human timelines. Palantir’s ShipOS has turned a 200-hour production process into 12 seconds for the Navy. In e-commerce fulfillment centers, Mercado Libre is deploying Agility’s Digit robots , while Shanghai’s RobotGym is sending humanoids into nursing homes .

Silicon is eating the capital stack. Oracle’s backlog has soared 438% to $523 billion on data center demand. To keep up, Supermicro and xAI are constructing the world’s first gigawatt data center in Memphis. But the cost is shifting: Epoch AI estimates that memory now accounts for nearly half the manufacturing cost of an NVIDIA B200 GPU, relegating the logic die to a minor line item. To optimize the next generation, Elon Musk confirms Tesla is now using Grok to design its chips , and Unsloth AI has released kernels that train open-weight models 3-5x faster .

We are industrializing the planet’s geology and atmosphere. Fervo Energy raised $462 million to scale geothermal for AI power, while Rainmaker Technology is promising weather modification to ensure year-round skiing and swimmable lakes in the American West.

Orbit is becoming a compute layer. Elon Musk plans to scale Starlink satellites to >100 kW to focus on AI compute , while Blue Origin now has a dedicated team for space-based data centers . Starcloud has successfully conducted the first training run of an LLM in orbit and hosted Google’s existing Gemma model on a satellite. Starlab has given us a detailed tour of its three-story cylindrical commercial habitat launching in 2029 , featuring 1,600 square feet of floor area.

We are uplifting the rest of the biosphere. Project CETI has released WhAM , a model that generates synthetic whale codas, allowing us to speak to the ocean. The CDC is ending monkey research to pivot to organ chips.

Gene therapy is becoming a charitable enterprise. The FDA has approved the first nonprofit gene therapy for a rare genetic disease, decoupling life-saving patches from the profit motive.

The economy is pricing in the inevitable. Polymarket gives Elon a 32% chance of becoming a trillionaire by the end of 2026 . Amazon is investing $35 billion in AI for India , while the House NDAA mandates a Pentagon panel to study the military implications of AGI . Ornn reports that it has executed the first GPU compute swap , financializing teraflops as a tradeable commodity.

Compute is the new gold standard.
