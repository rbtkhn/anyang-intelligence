Competitors are retreating to specific niches. Blue Origin will pause New Shepard flights for two years to focus entirely on the Moon. Meanwhile, the EU has switched on its own sovereign satellite network to wean itself off American constellations.

The simulation is rendering faster than we can watch it. xAI released Grok Imagine 1.0, which generates 10-second 720p clips with improved audio, and has already created 1.2 billion videos in just 30 days. Rumors suggest Claude Sonnet 5 and Nano Banana Flash 2 are imminent. Nostalgia is now a prompt. An Andreessen Horowitz partner used Genie 3 to recreate a 2000s-era Blockbuster complete with an unrequested Matrix VHS cassette, a simulation handing a user a movie about living in a simulation. Agents are also mastering leisure. GPT-5.2 beat Pokémon Emerald after spending 71% of its time thinking.

The financial system is attempting to price in superintelligence. Oracle plans to raise up to $50 billion for AI cloud capacity. Jensen Huang says Nvidia will join OpenAI’s $100 billion funding round in potentially its largest investment ever . Enterprise adoption is saturating. A survey reports 78% of Global 2000 CIOs now use OpenAI.

The compute bottleneck has shifted from silicon to manual labor. Record demand for fiber-optic cables has created a shortage of 178,000 workers needed to wire the hive mind. Meanwhile, we are mastering the thermodynamics of computing at the quantum scale. Swedish researchers achieved the first quantum Brownian refrigerator to control heat in quantum circuits.

We are optimizing everything from code to digestion. Linux developer Chris Mason released AI prompts for kernel review , a critical step in the recursive self-improvement loop where AI improves the operating system it runs on. Biology is getting the same treatment. OpenAI is working with pharma to unblock clinical discovery , while China is even backing AI for traditional medicine diagnosis.

The physical footprint of retail is mutating. Costco found it cheaper to build 800 apartments over a store in California than to build just the store, heralding a new form factor for big box retail. Food delivery has taken over dining. Three out of four restaurant meals are now eaten outside restaurants.

The energy transition has won. The EIA projects renewables and batteries will supply 99.2% of new US electric capacity in 2026. Tesla has achieved a major breakthrough with dry electrode production at scale . Walmart is adding EV charging to its parking lots in 19 states . Waymo is closing its latest round at a $110 billion valuation . Meanwhile, transportation is leaving the ground. Pivotal is taking deposits for its $190,000 flying car , expected to ship between April and May.
