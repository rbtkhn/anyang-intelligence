# External Interviews ROI Ledger

This ledger tracks extraction quality by seam across external interviews.

The purpose is to make it obvious what each source actually produced for Anyang Intelligence:

- a primitive candidate
- a verification queue
- a lane-safe routing packet
- or worldview context only

## ROI Disposition Rules

| Disposition | Use when |
| --- | --- |
| `primitive-candidate` | The seam yields a reusable gate, checklist, template, or validator that could apply across at least 2 lanes or is likely to recur across external interviews |
| `needs-verification` | The best implication depends on unstable benchmark, company, policy, legal, technical, pricing, or timeline claims |
| `lane-test-ready` | The seam can be translated into a lane-safe review question or checklist without making a new factual claim |
| `worldview-only` | The seam is strategically interesting but not yet reusable or safe to route |
| `preserved` | The primitive or routing artifact is already repo-visible elsewhere |
