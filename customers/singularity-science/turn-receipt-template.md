# Turn Receipt Template

Use this template when one bounded AI-assisted turn produces a real workflow outcome that should be reviewable later.

The point is to record what the turn actually absorbed, what it produced, where approval stopped it, and whether the result functioned like a small workflow rather than polished text.

## Boundary

The receipt is an evaluation artifact, not an approval record by itself.

Do not use the receipt to imply:

- customer approval
- parent approval
- owner approval
- board approval
- publication approval
- rights clearance
- professional review completion
