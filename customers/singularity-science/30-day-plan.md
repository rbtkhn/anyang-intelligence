# Singularity Science 30-Day Plan

This plan turns the research lane into a repeatable product-intelligence workflow over the next 30 days.

## Week 1: Archive and Review Baseline

- Confirm archive completeness for active source lanes.
- Identify the next 3 highest-value source analyses to complete.
- Review current primitive candidates and watch items.
- Confirm that transcript intake, source notes, analyses, and ledgers are all findable.

## Week 2: Primitive Preservation

- Convert repeated research signals into preserved primitive candidates.
- Tighten routing docs around ambient agency, turn usefulness, and research-to-lane transfer.
- Identify one candidate that should move from watch to tested.

## Week 3: Receiving-Lane Application

- Apply one Singularity Science primitive to one receiving lane without moving transcript bulk.
- Record what remained advisory, what required approval, and what stayed fully local.
- Capture the first preserved evidence of research-to-lane application quality.

## Week 4: Review and Calibration

- Run the research operating review.
- Review which analyses were most useful for product intelligence.
- Update the risk register and decision log based on real routing pressure.
- Decide the next source lane or primitive focus for the following 30 days.
