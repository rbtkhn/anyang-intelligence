# Singularity Science Membrane Notes

These notes describe how Singularity Science transfers insight without transferring unsafe source material.

## Transfer Boundary

Allowed transfer:

- original synthesis
- primitive candidates
- lane-safe review questions
- governance warnings
- membrane-aware product implications

Blocked transfer without explicit human review:

- long transcript passages
- source-specific rhetoric presented as Anyang Intelligence doctrine
- customer obligations derived directly from research enthusiasm
- rights-uncertain source material

## Private Boundary

Private or lane-local material includes:

- raw transcripts and transcript-heavy source captures
- rights or attribution uncertainty
- source-specific analyses not yet translated into generic primitives
- any customer-routing draft that still embeds source-specific phrasing

## Working Rule

Singularity Science may transfer synthesized implications.

It may not transfer private source bulk, rights-uncertain material, or unreviewed authority into customer or public lanes.
