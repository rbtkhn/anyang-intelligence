# Approval Receipt Template

Use this template when a human approval step materially changes whether a workflow outcome may be used, routed, published, shared, or treated as final.

The point is to make approval quality visible so a workflow does not confuse a polished draft with a real authority checkpoint.

Use with:

- [approval-quality-note.md](approval-quality-note.md)
- [turn-receipt-template.md](turn-receipt-template.md)
- [orchestration-failure-mode-ledger.md](orchestration-failure-mode-ledger.md)

## Template

```text
Approval Receipt

Lane:
Workflow surface:
Date:

Approver role:

Authority actually held:
-

-

-

-

- approved as-is
- approved with changes
- provisional / partial approval
- held
- rejected
- escalated for professional or higher authority review

Changes required before use:
-

- 

Meaningful-approval assessment:
- strong
- mixed
- weak / symbolic

Why the approval was meaningful or symbolic:

Next required follow-up:
- 
```

## Boundary

This receipt preserves an approval event. It does not replace the authority itself.

Do not use the existence of an approval receipt to imply:

- legal clearance
- rights clearance
- professional certification
- board action already completed
- parent consent for broader future uses
- owner permission for unrelated future changes
