# Elementary School

Elementary School is a K-5 educational operations installation for the Anyang Intelligence Executive Operating System.

## Organization

Elementary School is a high-trust educational operating context:

- Work: student learning, classroom operations, family communication, staff coordination, safety, scheduling, interventions, and school improvement.
- Cadence: daily school operations, weekly staff coordination, grading periods, family communication cycles, safety drills, and school-year planning.
- Operating challenge: support students, teachers, families, and administrators without compromising privacy, safety, professional judgment, or care.

## Executive OS Role

Anyang Intelligence acts as the administrative and coordination layer for school leadership.

In practical terms, the Executive OS helps:

- Maintain operational memory for school priorities, calendars, programs, and follow-ups.
- Coordinate student support workflows without replacing educator judgment.
- Prepare staff meeting agendas, family communication drafts, and leadership reviews.
- Track interventions, classroom needs, safety actions, and resource gaps at an appropriate privacy level.
- Surface patterns that require human review.
- Preserve decisions, responsibilities, and learning across the school year.

The system does not replace teachers, administrators, counselors, nurses, special education professionals, families, or mandated reporting processes. It supports responsible human coordination.

## Operating Thesis

An elementary school should not rely on scattered memory and informal follow-up for high-trust work.

The Executive OS should help the school stay coherent while keeping children protected and humans accountable:

```text
student and school signals
  -> human review
  -> support plan or operational action
  -> staff / family coordination
  -> follow-up
  -> evidence and reflection
  -> next school decision
```
