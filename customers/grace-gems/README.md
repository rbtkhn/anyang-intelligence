# Grace Gems

Grace Gems is the first internal installation target for the Anyang Intelligence Executive Operating System.

## Business

Grace Gems is a dual-storefront Etsy jewelry business:

- GraceGemsUS: main U.S. trust and conversion storefront for custom gemstone jewelry, giftable pieces, and personalized fine jewelry.
- GioielloHandcrafted: artisan and design-forward storefront for boutique, handcrafted, one-of-one pieces.

## Executive OS Role

Anyang Intelligence acts as the executive operating layer for Grace Gems.

In practical terms, the Executive OS is responsible for keeping the business coherent:

- Maintain the current model of the business.
- Decide which products and listings deserve attention.
- Gate listings before publish or promotion.
- Protect margin, policy safety, and customer trust.
- Coordinate daily and weekly operating work.
- Convert customer-service signals into product and listing improvements.
- Preserve decisions, risks, and lessons learned.

The system does not replace human ownership. It functions as an AI executive partner that prepares decisions, coordinates execution, and keeps the business learning.

## Operating Thesis

Grace Gems should not compete as a generic Etsy seller.

It should operate as a margin-aware, policy-safe, search-aware, customer-promise-clear marketplace business.

```text
product idea
  -> listing draft
  -> margin + policy review
  -> search / conversion / customer-promise review
  -> publish / revise / hold
  -> marketplace operations
  -> customer-service feedback
  -> product pipeline update
```
