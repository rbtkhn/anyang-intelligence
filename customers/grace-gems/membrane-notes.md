# Grace Gems Membrane Notes

These notes describe how Grace Gems should transfer lessons while protecting owner, customer, and claim-sensitive context.

## Transfer Boundary

Allowed transfer:

- generic marketplace QA patterns
- listing-gate logic
- customer-confusion pattern handling
- owner-approval review questions

Blocked transfer without explicit owner approval:

- product-specific claims
- pricing or margin strategy
- customer identities or private messages
- shipping, repair, return, certificate, or authenticity promises

## Private Boundary

Private or lane-local material includes:

- buyer identities and order details
- customer-service transcripts
- product margin assumptions and pricing strategy
- owner-only storefront strategy

## Working Rule

Grace Gems may transfer generic marketplace patterns.

It may not transfer private customer context, private owner strategy, or unapproved product claims without explicit human approval.
