# Grace Gems 30-Day Plan

This plan creates the first recurring operating support surfaces for Grace Gems.

## Week 1: Control Surface Baseline

- Create the first risk register and decision log.
- Review the current storefront roles and listing inventory.
- Confirm the active listing gate workflow and owner approval boundaries.

## Week 2: Listing and Margin Review

- Run the listing gate on the highest-priority current or draft listings.
- Review margin assumptions, customer promise clarity, and no-ad candidates.
- Identify the weakest listings with meaningful improvement potential.

## Week 3: Customer Confusion Review

- Review repeated customer-service questions and confusion signals.
- Convert recurring issues into listing corrections, FAQ notes, or hold decisions.
- Update the decision log for any repeated promise or policy issue.

## Week 4: Operating Review and Focus Reset

- Run the first full weekly-style operating review as a monthly checkpoint.
- Update the risk register from real listing and customer pressure.
- Choose the next 30-day storefront, listing, or trust-clarity focus.
