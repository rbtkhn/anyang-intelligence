## Working Interpretation

Grace Gems is not primarily competing as a low-cost jewelry shop.

The stronger positioning is:

```text
trusted online fine jeweler
  -> natural gemstone expertise
  -> custom and personalized solid-gold jewelry
  -> buyer confidence through proof, service, and clear promises
```

The public shop promise stack includes:

- Natural and untreated gemstone claims.
- Optional appraisal certificate service.
- Free worldwide shipping.
- Responsive customer help.
- Layaway/payment plan availability.
- Return policy with custom-item exception.
- One-year repair warranty.
- Complimentary jewelry box.

This creates a clear operating opportunity: buyer trust is the product wrapper around the jewelry.

These assets should be designed for reuse across listings, WeChat/social selling, customer support replies, promotion pages, and owner review.

## Claim Discipline Risks

The strongest public claims are also the highest-risk claims. No creative asset should independently restate, expand, or modify these without owner approval:

- "Natural" or "untreated" gemstone status.
- Appraisal/certificate availability, price, or timeline.
- Gold purity, solid-gold status, or no gold-plated positioning.
- Returns, refund eligibility, repair warranty, custom-item exceptions.
- Shipping cost, shipping speed, worldwide shipping, import taxes, or delay responsibility.
- Rush order timing and cost.
- Any promise of 24/7 support or response speed.
- Any claim that a stone is rare, certified, investment-grade, ethical, untreated, earth-mined, or from a specific origin.

Default rule: turn claims into owner-review questions before turning them into public copy.

## First Creative Deliverables

The first Grace Gems creative backlog should start with trust and support assets rather than random product promotion.

Recommended first-week deliverables:

1. **Trust Stack One-Pager** - a visual buyer-facing summary of natural gemstones, solid gold, custom work, shipping, returns, repair warranty, and certificate option.
2. **Custom Order Journey Graphic** - a simple process asset explaining inquiry, stone/design selection, quote, approval, production, shipping, and aftercare.
3. **Appraisal Certificate FAQ Draft** - owner-reviewed language explaining when a certificate is available, what it costs, how long it adds, and what it does or does not prove.
4. **Return And Repair FAQ Draft** - owner-reviewed support language for standard items, custom-item exceptions, return window, and one-year repair warranty.
5. **Listing Image Trust Badge Set** - small reusable visual badges such as "solid 14k/18k gold," "custom handmade," "certificate available," and "free worldwide shipping," subject to owner approval.

## What Still Requires Owner Intake

Do not proceed from public evidence alone on:

- True bestsellers and highest-priority products.
- Margins, costs, discounts, and profitability by category.
- Which claims are legally, operationally, and factually supportable.
- Current customer-support pain points.
- Which listing images currently underperform.
- Production capacity and fulfillment constraints.
- Owner approval boundaries for customer replies, policy copy, promotions, discounts, and product claims.

The owner/operator intake remains required before finalizing public-facing assets.

Until better internal data arrives, Grace Gems creative support should use this public shop signal to focus on buyer trust, custom-order clarity, and claim-safe support content.
