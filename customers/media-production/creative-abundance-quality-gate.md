# Creative Abundance Quality Gate

Use this gate before a Media Production asset is treated as ready for client review, publication, or delivery.

The goal is to confirm that the asset creates useful abundance: clarity, trust, reuse, learning, and lower friction.

## 1. Strategic Fit

- What client need does this asset serve?
- What decision, sale, lesson, or customer/audience action should become easier?
- Is this asset tied to an approved brief, product need, or production priority?

Pass condition: the asset has a clear job.

## 2. Abundance Check

What does this asset make more abundant?

- Buyer confidence.
- Product clarity.
- Audience understanding.
- Customer support capacity.
- Reusable creative memory.
- Sales support.
- Community participation.
- Calm execution.
- Other:

Pass condition: the asset creates identifiable value beyond existing as content.

## 3. Accuracy And Trust

- Are product details, historical claims, prices, dimensions, timelines, or commitments accurate?
- Is anything uncertain clearly flagged?
- Does the asset avoid exaggeration or invented claims?
- Are source notes, product references, or approval needs recorded?

Pass condition: no material claim is unsupported or misleading.

## 4. Reuse And Compounding

- Can this asset be reused, adapted, or split into another useful format?
- Does the file name make reuse easy?
- Are source files, prompts, captions, or notes saved where future work can find them?
- Is there a next asset this should generate?

Pass condition: the asset can compound instead of disappearing after one use.

## 5. Audience Or Customer Usefulness

- Would this help a real customer, buyer, viewer, or community member?
- Is the message readable on the intended device or channel?
- Is the hook clear without being misleading?
- Does the asset reduce confusion?

Pass condition: the asset is useful to someone outside the production process.

## 6. Capacity And Budget

- Does this fit Cici's current workload?
- Does it require any paid tool, asset, contractor, or spend?
- If spending is needed, has a human approved it?
- Does this asset create enough value to justify the effort?

Pass condition: the work fits current capacity and budget constraints.
