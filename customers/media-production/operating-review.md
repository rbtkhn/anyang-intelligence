# Media Production Operating Review

This is the recurring operating review surface for Media Production.

Cadence: weekly, plus event-driven review before large assignments, unusual spend, or major publication decisions.

## Review Questions

### Production Flow

- What shipped?
- What is in production?
- What is blocked or waiting on review?
- Which assignments need revision before contractor handoff?

### Client and Lane Balance

- What Grace Gems deliverables need action next?
- What Predictive History support work is ready or delayed?
- Is the lane balancing paid support and creative development coherently?

### Quality and Rights

- Which outputs passed the quality gate cleanly?
- Which assets raised rights, factual, or claim concerns?
- Where did review catch issues late rather than early?

### Capacity and Budget

- How much contractor capacity is already committed?
- How much of the discretionary monthly budget remains?
- Which work created reusable abundance and which work was one-off?

### Learning

- Which hooks, formats, or channels worked best?
- What should be repurposed?
- What should ship next?

### Turn Usefulness Review

- Which turn created the strongest reviewable production progress this cycle?
- Which turn looked polished but still hid cleanup in the brief, rights, or review packet?
- Where did the system stop too early and leave avoidable coordination work for the operator?
- Where did any turn get too close to implying publication, client commitment, or rights approval?
- Which repeated failure mode should become a tighter template, gate, or contractor instruction?
- Where did a workflow look complete but still stall on permissions, rights, spend, or approval authority?
