# Media Production Risk Register

This register tracks the main operating and governance risks for Media Production.

Review cadence: weekly operating review and before any unusual spend, contractor assignment, or publication-sensitive delivery.

| Risk | Consequence | Owner | Current posture | Next review |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Creative Production Operator work is assigned without a review-ready brief | Contractor output drifts, rework increases, and client trust falls | Anyang Intelligence operator | Use the assignment gate before every task handoff | Weekly operating review |
| Rights or factual uncertainty is missed before publication | Published assets may create claim, attribution, or legal risk | Anyang Intelligence operator | Keep quality gate and rights review explicit before publication | Before each publish-ready asset |
| Grace Gems work crowds out Predictive History or vice versa | Production becomes reactive and loses strategic coherence | Anyang Intelligence operator | Use lane-aware backlog and calendar review | Weekly operating review |
| The $500 discretionary budget is spent without clear abundance value | Small-budget production capacity gets exhausted with low learning return | Anyang Intelligence operator | Review spend against expected asset reuse and output quality | Weekly budget review |
| Contractor capacity is treated as larger than proven | Deadlines, quality, and review discipline degrade | Anyang Intelligence operator | Default to small scope until capacity is demonstrated | Before new assignment set |
