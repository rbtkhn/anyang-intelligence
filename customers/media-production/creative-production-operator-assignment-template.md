## Do Not Invent

For Grace Gems, do not invent:

- product details
- prices
- dimensions
- materials
- gemstone identity
- authenticity claims
- shipping promises
- repair, return, warranty, certificate, or appraisal language
- discounts or promotion terms
- customer commitments

For Predictive History, do not invent:

- historical claims
- quotes
- dates
- maps or territorial boundaries
- source provenance
- likeness rights
- music or image rights
- episode conclusions not present in source

## Quality Gate Before Return

Before returning the work, the CPO should check:

- The asset has a clear job.
- The work matches the source signal.
- No unsupported claim was added.
- Rights or source uncertainty is flagged.
- The asset is useful to a buyer, customer, viewer, or operator.
- The file is organized for review and reuse.
- Any spend or tool need is flagged before use.

Use [creative-abundance-quality-gate.md](creative-abundance-quality-gate.md) before treating work as review-ready.

## Human Authority Boundary

This assignment authorizes draft production only.

It does not authorize:

- publication
- client delivery
- customer messages
- final claims
- spending
- rights decisions
- subcontracting
- final approval

Human review is required before delivery, publication, spend, external claims, or client commitments.
