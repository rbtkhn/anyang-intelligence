# Creative Abundance Ledger Template

Use this ledger to record creative work that compounds across assets, channels, customer support, sales, learning, or community.

The ledger answers: what understanding did we turn into reusable value?

## Ledger

| Date | Client | Insight / source signal | Assets created | Reused where | Outcome / signal | Next action |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| YYYY-MM-DD | Grace Gems | Customer asks whether a product is natural stone | FAQ answer; listing note; WeChat post; short video idea | Product listing; chat response; social post | Fewer repeated explanations; buyer trust signal pending | Add to product support library |
| YYYY-MM-DD | Predictive History | Topic has a strong visual contrast between prediction and outcome | Thumbnail concept; quote card; short clip hook; lesson visual | YouTube; Shorts; archive notes | Audience response pending | Compare performance after publish |

## Entry Checklist

For each entry, record:

- The source signal: customer question, product issue, audience signal, topic insight, owner decision, or recurring friction.
- The assets created.
- Where the assets were reused.
- What outcome or signal appeared.
- What should happen next.

## Monthly Review Questions

- Which assets were reused the most?
- Which customer or audience questions repeated?
- Which creative patterns reduced friction?
- Which assets failed to create value?
- What should become a template?
- What should be stopped?
