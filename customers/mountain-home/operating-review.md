# Mountain Home Operating Review

This is the recurring operating review surface for Mountain Home.

Cadence: weekly during active risk windows, monthly when conditions are stable, and before each seasonal transition.

## Review Questions

### Top Risks

- What are the top 5 property risks right now?
- Which risks threaten habitability, insurability, access, water, heat, or asset value?
- Which risks are rising because of weather or deferred maintenance?

### Continuity

- How does the property currently stand against the 72-hour continuity standard?
- Which continuity gaps still lack mitigation or documentation?

### Maintenance and Escalation

- Which maintenance items are blocked?
- Which issues now require contractor, inspection, or professional review?
- What proof or receipts are still missing?

### Seasonal Readiness

- Which seasonal gate is next?
- What must be done before that season arrives?

### Next Actions

- What should be done next?
- What should be scheduled next?
- What must stay on hold pending owner or professional judgment?

### Turn Usefulness Review

- Which turn created the clearest risk-reduction forward motion this cycle?
- Which turn looked complete but still hid cleanup around proof, escalation, or seasonal readiness?
- Where did the system stop too early and leave the owner to rebuild the action path?
- Where did any recommendation get too close to implying safety, insurance, contractor, or access authority?
- Which repeated failure mode should become a tighter checklist, receipt, or escalation gate?
