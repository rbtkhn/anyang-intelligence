# Mountain Home Membrane Notes

These notes describe how Mountain Home should transfer lessons while protecting property-sensitive context.

## Transfer Boundary

Allowed transfer:

- generic risk-ranking patterns
- seasonal-readiness patterns
- continuity review questions
- proof-of-mitigation discipline

Blocked transfer without explicit owner approval:

- exact access details
- property vulnerabilities
- insurance-sensitive details
- security-sensitive photos, maps, or notes

## Private Boundary

Private or lane-local material includes:

- property access constraints
- utility weaknesses and continuity gaps
- insurance and valuation-sensitive records
- contractor quotes, inspections, receipts, maps, and proof photos

## Working Rule

Mountain Home may transfer generic property stewardship patterns.

It may not transfer private property details, vulnerabilities, or insurance-sensitive context without explicit human approval.
