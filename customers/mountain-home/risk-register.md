# Mountain Home Risk Register

This register tracks the main operating and governance risks for Mountain Home.

Review cadence: weekly during active risk windows, seasonally for major transitions, and event-driven for safety or continuity issues.

| Risk | Consequence | Owner | Current posture | Next review |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| High-consequence property risks are treated as routine chores | Critical continuity and safety issues are deferred too long | Property owner | Use risk-ranked review before optional projects | Weekly risk review |
| Wildfire mitigation proof is incomplete | Real work is harder to verify for insurance, review, or learning | Property owner | Record proof-of-mitigation expectations explicitly | Before peak fire season |
| Water, septic, or access issues stay undocumented until failure | Continuity and repair decisions become reactive | Property owner | Keep continuity and inspection gaps visible in review | Weekly review during active seasons |
| Contractor or professional-review thresholds are unclear | Owner waits too long on issues outside safe DIY range | Property owner | Escalate when inspection, spending, or safety uncertainty rises | Event-driven |
| Seasonal readiness review happens after urgency arrives | Snow, fire, mud, or utility pressures compress response options | Property owner | Use seasonal loop before each risk season | Before each seasonal gate |
