# Product

## Product Category

Owner-governed intelligence systems built on a shared operating substrate.

## Product Family

- **Personal AI Agent:** an emerging owner-facing continuity offering that coordinates purposes, memory, successive models, specialized agents, and membrane-separated project contexts across life and work. The current repository is its seed and operating architecture; it is not yet represented as a production-mature autonomous agent.
- **Operational OS:** the current organizational offering. It is implemented through an AI-native Executive Operating System installed inside a business, project, or organization.

Both offerings use the same evidence, memory, routing, review, and authority primitives. They do not share private facts or approvals merely because they share infrastructure. See the canonical [continuity intent](operating-substrate.md#core-thesis).

The first commercial product remains a service-led Operational OS installation that combines structured discovery, operating design, AI configuration, and leadership enablement.

## Strategic Direction: Embodied Agency

Anyang Intelligence builds governed intelligence systems that help people understand, coordinate, and eventually act in digital and physical worlds. The Executive OS is the control layer: it preserves context, frames decisions, records approvals, verifies outcomes, and escalates exceptions before a system is trusted with a wider scope of action.

Embodied agency does not mean autonomous authority. Any tool, automation, or physical system remains limited to defined tasks and may not make safety-critical, financial, legal, employment, or other consequential decisions without the appropriate human approval.

## Target Customer

The initial Operational OS customer is a founder-led business or lean organization with roughly 5 to 100 people.

They have enough complexity to need executive infrastructure, but not enough staff or process maturity to justify a full chief of staff, strategy office, or operations team.

The emerging Personal AI Agent is owner-facing. Its current purpose is continuity and coordination for an operator with multiple responsibilities and projects, not general consumer automation or an assertion of independent agency.

## Core Jobs

- Convert scattered context into a coherent business model.
- Turn leadership ambiguity into clear decision options.
- Convert decisions into owners, milestones, reviews, and follow-ups.
- Track assumptions and compare expected outcomes against actual outcomes.
- Preserve institutional memory across meetings, projects, and leadership cycles.

## Initial Deliverables

- Company context map.
- Executive memory model.
- Decision log.
- Weekly executive review.
- Risk register.
- Project and owner tracker.
- Decision memo workflow.
- Learning loop for assumptions and outcomes.
- Loop grammar for repeatable memory, decision, coordination, review, learning, and governance cycles.

## Product Boundary

The Executive OS supports leadership judgment. It does not make legally binding decisions, replace human accountability, control funds, hire or fire employees, or execute irreversible actions without human approval.

The Personal AI Agent follows the same boundary. Model succession, broader context, or coordination across specialized agents does not transfer approvals, expand authority, or make project-local information globally available.

Future physical or embodied deployments must additionally meet task-usefulness, supervision, maintenance, security, privacy, insurance, and override requirements before adoption.
