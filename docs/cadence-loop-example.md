# Cadence Loop Example

This example shows how native `coffee`, `bravo`, and `dream` work together as a recursive self-enhancement loop in Anyang Intelligence.

The fixture is the recent creation of the native cadence skills themselves.

## Loop Shape

```text
coffee
  -> choose the next improvement

work
  -> preserve the improvement in a repo-visible surface

bravo
  -> capture what worked and what pattern should be reinforced

dream
  -> confirm what landed and what tomorrow inherits

next coffee
  -> re-enter with the improved operating surface
```

## Fixture: Native Cadence Skills

### Coffee

Signal:

- The operator asked for orientation and next action.

What coffee did:

- Restored repo context.
- Named the live obligations and entropy.
- Identified a durable improvement candidate: make the closeout and positive-signal rituals native to Anyang Intelligence instead of relying on strategy-codex memory.

Learning:

- Anyang Intelligence needed its own cadence layer, grounded in `repo_probe`, customer folders, skills, and native CLIs.

### Work

Action:

- Added [coffee](../skills/coffee/SKILL.md), [dream](../skills/dream/SKILL.md), and [bravo](../skills/bravo/SKILL.md) as native skills.
- Linked them from [skills/README.md](../skills/README.md).
- Connected them to [recursive-self-enhancement.md](recursive-self-enhancement.md) and [operating-substrate.md](operating-substrate.md).

Evidence:

- The skills exist in repo-visible files.
- The docs name the cadence roles.
- Native validation passed for customer installs and loop examples.

### Bravo

Signal:

- The operator said `bravo` after the native `bravo` skill landed.

What bravo captured:

- The useful pattern was not praise itself.
- The useful pattern was converting a felt operator signal into a small native skill with guardrails, doctrine links, validation, commit, and push.

Reinforced pattern:

- When a conversational intuition has clear operating value, encode it as a narrow skill or checklist, then link it only where it improves future work.

Boundary:

- Do not convert every satisfying moment into doctrine.
- Do not generalize praise into authority.
- Do not preserve customer-specific or private context as shared product doctrine.

### Dream

Signal:

- The work cycle ended after several native cadence and training-surface improvements.

What dream confirmed:

- The repo was clean and synced.
- Customer install validation passed with known legacy warnings only.
- Loop validation passed.
- The closeout should not invent unavailable `last-dream.json`, cadence logs, or strategy-codex scripts.

Learning:

- Dream is the consolidation half of recursive improvement: it verifies what landed, separates fresh issues from known noise, and names what tomorrow inherits.

### Next Coffee

Signal:

- The next `coffee` began after `bravo` and `dream` were native.

What coffee inherited:

- A cleaner cadence model:

```text
coffee detects
work changes
bravo reinforces
dream consolidates
coffee inherits
```

Next best use:

- Apply the cadence trio to real customer work, especially Media Production / Grace Gems or Elementary School parent-intake artifacts, rather than continuing to build meta-cadence indefinitely.

## Why This Matters

The system did not merely remember a good interaction. It changed the operating surface so future agents have a procedure for:

- choosing improvements from friction
- reinforcing improvements from satisfaction
- confirming what landed before the next cycle

That is recursive self-enhancement in the Anyang Intelligence sense: human-approved, repo-visible, bounded, validated improvement to the system that will handle the next similar work cycle.

## Anti-Pattern

Do not use this cadence loop to produce infinite meta-work.

The loop is valuable only when it improves future customer or product work. The next proof should come from applying it to a concrete lane, not from endlessly elaborating the cadence itself.
