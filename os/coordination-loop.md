# Coordination Loop

## Purpose

The coordination loop converts decisions into execution.

It tracks who owns what, what changed, where work is blocked, and what needs leadership attention.

## Core Objects

- Owner.
- Project.
- Milestone.
- Dependency.
- Blocker.
- Next action.
- Review date.
- Status narrative.

## Weekly Rhythm

- Review current priorities.
- Check active projects.
- Surface blockers and dependency issues.
- Update risks.
- Confirm next actions.
- Capture decisions.
- Schedule follow-up reviews.

## Output

The coordination loop should produce a weekly operating review that leadership can scan quickly and act on immediately.
