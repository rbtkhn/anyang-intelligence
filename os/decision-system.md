# Decision System

## Purpose

The decision system turns ambiguity into structured executive judgment.

It should help leaders see options, tradeoffs, constraints, risks, and next steps without pretending the AI has authority to decide alone.

## Decision Memo Shape

- Decision to make.
- Context.
- Options.
- Tradeoffs.
- Source evidence.
- Assumptions.
- Risks.
- Recommendation.
- Approval needed.
- Follow-up review date.

## Decision Types

- Strategic priority.
- Resource allocation.
- Hiring or team design.
- Customer or market choice.
- Product direction.
- Operational change.
- Risk response.

## Standard

A good decision artifact reduces confusion, names what is uncertain, and makes the next human action obvious.
